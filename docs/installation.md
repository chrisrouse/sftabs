# Installation

## Supported Browsers

SF Tabs is available for Firefox and Chrome browsers. Choose your browser below to get started.


## Installation Steps

SF Tabs is available for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge browsers. Click on the name of your preferred browser below to go to the extension list.

Apple charges $99/year to list an extension, including free extensions. If you are already an Apple developer and would like to make SF Tabs available, you are free to fork the extension and distribute it for free.

## Required Permissions

SF Tabs requires the following permissions to function:

- **Access your data for sites matching:** `*.salesforce.com`, `*.force.com` - Required to inject custom tabs into Salesforce Setup pages
- **Store data on your device** - Required to save your custom tab configuration
- **Access browser tabs** - Required to open tabs when you click on them

After installing SF Tabs in your browser, you may need to allow permissions for the plugin
