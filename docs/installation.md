# Installation

## Installation Steps

SF Tabs is available for Firefox and Chrome, as well as Chromium-based browsers that can use Chrome extensions. Click on the name of your preferred browser below to go to the extension list.

[![Firefox](https://img.shields.io/badge/Firefox-FF7139?style=for-the-badge&logo=firefox&logoColor=white)](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/sf-tabs/)
[![Chrome](https://img.shields.io/badge/Chrome-4285F4?style=for-the-badge&logo=googlechrome&logoColor=white)](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/sf-tabs/lkimhffllnjkacnhjfehaihcjilcmdlo)


## Required Permissions

SF Tabs requires the following permissions to function:

- **Access your data for sites matching:** `*.salesforce.com`, `*.force.com` - Required to inject custom tabs into Salesforce Setup pages
- **Store data on your device** - Required to save your custom tab configuration
- **Access browser tabs** - Required to open tabs when you click on them

After installing SF Tabs in your browser, you may need to allow permissions for the plugin. Your browser should prompt you to allow the necessary permissions. If not, visit your browser's extension pages.
